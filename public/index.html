The Artist

About Saiteja

2017

Behind the Lens

"I don't just shoot weddings —
I preserve emotions for lifetimes."

Based in Karimnagar and serving all across Telangana, I am Saiteja — a wedding and events photographer with a passion for cinematic storytelling. Every wedding I photograph is a sacred responsibility, a privilege to witness love at its most vulnerable and beautiful.

With over 8 years of experience capturing Telugu weddings, I understand the depth of our traditions, the grandeur of our ceremonies, and the intimacy of family moments that happen between the rituals. My camera finds what the eye might miss.

My style blends documentary authenticity with cinematic artistry — real moments, beautifully rendered. I work quietly, move intuitively, and never let a camera come between you and your feelings.

— Saiteja

The Journey

What Drives My Work

01

Cinematic Vision

Every frame is composed like a scene from a film — with intention, depth, and narrative weight. I see your wedding as a story unfolding in light and shadow.

02

Cultural Roots

Born and raised in Telangana, I carry a deep respect for Telugu wedding traditions. I know exactly when to be ready for the most sacred moments.

03

Human Connection

My couples say they forgot I was there — that's the greatest compliment. I blend into your world so you can feel free to simply love each other.

What We Offer

Our Services

01

Wedding Photography

Full-day coverage of your sacred day — from the morning rituals to the final farewell. Every tear, laugh, and touch, preserved forever in cinematic frames.

  • Full day coverage (Muhurtham to Reception)
  • Candid & traditional photography
  • Professional edited gallery (300+ images)
  • Online delivery + USB drive
  • Pre-wedding consultation

Custom packages — best price guaranteed

02

Pre-Wedding Shoot

A relaxed, cinematic session for just the two of you — in locations that tell your story. Sunrise in paddy fields, heritage monuments, or urban art walls.

  • 4–6 hour shoot session
  • 2–3 curated locations
  • 100+ professionally edited images
  • Short cinematic film (optional)
  • Outfit change assistance

Flexible packages for every dream

03

Cinematic Wedding Film

A full cinematic film of your wedding day — edited with music, colour-grading, and storytelling that brings every moment back to life every time you watch.

  • 4K video capture
  • Feature film (8–12 mins)
  • Highlight reel (2–3 mins)
  • Drone aerial footage (select locations)
  • Professional colour grading

Ask us for a sample film

04

Events & Functions

Engagement ceremonies, Mehendi, Haldi, Sangeet, Baby showers, Birthday milestones — every celebration in your family deserves beautiful memories.

  • Coverage of all Telugu functions
  • Candid & posed photography
  • Same-day preview images
  • Quick turnaround delivery
  • Group & family portrait sessions
